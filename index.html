Plane Fun Flying Club

We are 10 pilots who fly a Cessna T182R (N2017E) out of Pearson Field (KVUO) in Vancouver, Washington.

Pearson is a great airfield for anyone who lives in the Portland metro area.

We're currently looking for additional members who would like to help us procure our second airplane, likely something similar to a DA-40 or a Cirrus SR-20.
